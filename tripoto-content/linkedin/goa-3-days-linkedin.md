## Version A (Business + Travel)

The most valuable thing I do for my business every quarter:

Take 3 days off in Goa.

Not to "network." Not to attend a conference. To actually stop.

Here's the counterintuitive part — the 3 days I spend with a scooter, no schedule, and no Wi-Fi consistently generate my best ideas.

Last trip, I figured out an approach to a product problem I'd been stuck on for 6 weeks. I wasn't trying to solve it. I was eating a ₹280 prawn curry on a plastic chair 200m from the beach. The answer just appeared.

There's research on this. The Default Mode Network (your brain's "rest mode") is where creative insight actually happens. It only activates when you're genuinely resting — not doom-scrolling, not "catching up on emails."

Genuine rest looks like:
→ A scooter on South Goa roads with no destination
→ A cold kingfisher on Palolem Beach at 4pm
→ Getting slightly lost in Fontainhas and not caring
→ A Goan sunset with no notifications

## Version B (Practical/Itinerary)

Most people do Goa wrong.

They go to Calangute, pay ₹800 for a beach shack meal, spend 4 days and come home saying "Goa is overrated."

Goa is not overrated. Their itinerary was.

The 3-day version that actually works:

**Day 1 — North Goa (right beaches, not Calangute):**
Vagator cliff viewpoint → Chapora Fort → Anjuna Flea Market → sunset cocktail at Antares → Baga for one evening (one evening is plenty)

**Day 2 — Old Goa (what most people skip entirely):**
Basilica of Bom Jesus (St Francis Xavier's 470-year-old preserved body) → Sé Cathedral (biggest church in Asia when built) → Fontainhas Latin Quarter (most photogenic street in Goa) → Feni tasting at a local bar in Panjim → ₹280 fish curry lunch at Mahalaxmi Restaurant (since 1938)

**Day 3 — South Goa (where the beach actually is):**
Palolem crescent beach at 7am → Butterfly Beach by boat → Agonda for swimming → Cabo de Rama Fort for sunset

Total cost: ₹6,400 including accommodation, scooter, and all meals.
Compared to: ₹12,000+ for the same number of days doing the standard tourist circuit.
