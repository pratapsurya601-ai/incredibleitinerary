## Engagement Protocol (After Each Post)

1. **Reply to every comment within 24 hours**
2. **Pin one "insider tip" comment on your own post** (adds value, bumps it in algorithm)
3. **Use Tripoto's Trip Capsule** feature to add photos for the same destination — links back to your story
4. **Share each post to your Instagram story** 24 hours after publishing

---

## Monthly Reposts (30 Days Later)

Tripoto allows reposts. Refresh the top 5 posts with:
- Updated prices
- New photos
- A "2026 Update:" note at the top

Suggested repost schedule:
- Day 31: Kashmir + Rajasthan
- Day 32: Kerala + Ladakh
- Day 33: Bali + Bangkok
- Day 34: Goa + Tokyo
- Day 35: Andaman + Dubai

## UTM Links to Add in Each Post

Add `?utm_source=tripoto&utm_medium=travel_story&utm_campaign=[slug]` to every link.

Example:
```
https://www.incredibleitinerary.com/blog/kashmir-6-days?utm_source=tripoto&utm_medium=travel_story&utm_campaign=kashmir
```

This lets you track exactly how much traffic Tripoto sends in Google Analytics.
